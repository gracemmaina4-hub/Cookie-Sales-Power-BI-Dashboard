# 🍪 Cookie Sales Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project showcases a **Cookie Sales Dashboard** built using **Power BI**, designed to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, and revenue trends.

The dashboard provides insights into key business metrics such as total revenue, profit, order volume, and best-selling products, helping stakeholders make informed decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall sales and profitability  
- Identify best-performing cookie products  
- Understand customer purchasing patterns  
- Track revenue trends over time  
- Evaluate order distribution across customers  

---

## 📂 Dataset Description
The dataset includes transactional sales data with the following key fields:

- 🛒 Orders (Order count, Order value)  
- 💰 Revenue and Profit  
- 🍪 Product types (e.g., Chocolate Chip, Oatmeal, etc.)  
- 👥 Customer data  
- 🌍 Customer location  
- 📅 Date (monthly and yearly breakdown)  

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### 💰 Sales Performance
- **Total Revenue:** $4.69M  
- **Total Profit:** $2.72M  
- **Average Order Value:** 6.70K  
- **Total Orders:** 700  

### 🍪 Product Insights
- **Best-Selling Cookie:** Chocolate Chip  
- Chocolate-based products generate the highest revenue  
- Lower-performing products include Sugar and Fortune Cookies  

### 👥 Customer Insights
- Revenue is distributed across multiple key customers  
- Top contributors include:
  - ACME Bites  
  - Wholesome Foods  
  - ABC Grocers  
  - Park & Shop  
  - Tres Delicios  

### 📈 Revenue Trends
- Revenue fluctuates throughout the year  
- Peak months show significant increases (e.g., mid-year and year-end)  
- Seasonal trends may influence sales performance  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features
- KPI cards:
  - Total Revenue  
  - Total Profit  
  - Average Order Value  
  - Total Orders  
  - Best-Selling Product  
- Interactive filters:
  - Cookie Type  
  - Customer Location  
  - Date  
- Revenue breakdown by product  
- Customer distribution (pie chart)  
- Monthly revenue trend analysis  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **Power BI** – Data visualization and dashboard creation  
- **Microsoft Excel** – Data preprocessing  

## 💡 Key Takeaways
- Chocolate Chip cookies dominate sales performance  
- Revenue shows seasonal fluctuations  
- A few key customers contribute significantly to revenue  
- Strong profitability with over 50% margin
